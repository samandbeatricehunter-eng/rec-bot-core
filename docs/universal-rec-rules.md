# Universal REC Rules

These rules are locked platform rules and should apply to every REC league.

## Conduct

- No racism, hate speech, threats, harassment, or doxxing.
- No account sharing.
- No playing games for another user.

## Gameplay Integrity

- No exploits.
- No glitch plays.
- No bug abuse.
- No hovering over the center to glitch blocking logic.
- No intentionally disconnecting to avoid losses.
- No manipulating imports.
- No stat padding against other users or the CPU.

Stat-padding indicators include:

- A 35+ point spread.
- 800+ yards of offense.

Exceptions may be granted when a game is legitimately high-scoring or unusually explosive.

## Scheduling

- Users must attempt to schedule games within the league's designated scheduling deadline posted upon each advance.
- Failure to do so can result in a force win being granted to the opponent at their request and commissioner discretion.
- Reasonable communication is required.
